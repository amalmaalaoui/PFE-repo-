Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.
What file formats are supported for migration?
We currently support CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), and JSON formats. Support for additional formats is coming soon.
How long do migrations typically take?
Migration time depends on the size of your data. Small files (under 1MB) usually complete in seconds, while larger files may take several minutes.
